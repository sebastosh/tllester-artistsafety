freeDimensional often shares its technical expertise in the field of free expression, conflict resolution and culture with new initiatives and projects, which then become featured Free Expression Projects on our homepage. Other examples of recent and upcoming leadership and training activities are:

- [Keynote presentation at the 2010 International Network for Contemporary Performing Arts (IETM) Autumn Plenary Meeting: Voices](http://www.ietm.org/index.lasso?p=information&q=eventdetail&id=424)
- [From Hosting to Hospitality in the Era of Globalization two-part panel at the 2010 Res Artis general meeting](http://www.resartis2010.rcaaq.org/program.php)
- [Arts and Political Change in Europe’s Last Dictatorship in the 2010 World Policy Institute / Heinrich Boll Stiftung Political Salon series with Belarus Free Theater](http://www.dramaturg.org)
- [Contributed to The Opportunity Agenda publication, Immigration: Arts, Culture and Media 2010 – A Creative Change Report](http://opportunityagenda.org/creativechangereport)
- [Represented at Itaú Cultural’s 2010 Antídoto International Seminar on Cultural Actions in Conflict Zones](http://www.itaucultural.org.br/antidoto2010/)
- [Inaugural interview on web archive, Conflict Alternatives](http://conflictalternatives.org/2010/06/03/freedimensional/)
- [Contributed to 2010 Sage publication, Cultures and Globalization 3: Cultural Expression, Creativity and Innovation](http://freedimensional.org/?attachment_id=89)
- [Artist Residencies as Sites of Research at the 2009 Istanbul Biennial with cura bodrum](http://www.curabodrumresidency.net)
- [Contributed to 2008 Commonwealth Foundation publication, Putting Culture First: Commonwealth perspectives on culture and development](http://www.commonwealthfoundation.com/LinkClick.aspx?fileticket=16LU0GdSEto%3d&tabid=247)

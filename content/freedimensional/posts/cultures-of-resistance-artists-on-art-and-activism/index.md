[![](images/2011-11-02-194x300.jpg "2011-11-02")](http://freedimensional.org/?attachment_id=1191)fD program coordinator and visual artist Sidd Joag joins a panel with poet Suheir Hammad, filmmaker Iara Lee and Paul D. Miller a.k.a D.J. Spooky, to explore the role of the artist in a global society, including that of the diasporic artist. The panel will be moderated by [http://www.raybandasoleit.com/](http://www.raybandasoleit.com/ "http://www.raybandasoleit.com/") NYU Tisch School of the Arts’ Arts and Public Policy Chair Randy Martin.  7-9PM @ NYU Casa Italiana Zerilli-Marimò

As part of the program, fD and Residency Unlimited will facilitate a working session on a new residency and resource mapping  initiative for artists-in-exile in New York City, including invited guests and open to the public. 3-5PM. @ NYU Institute for Public Knowledge

 

Event organized by the Asian/Pacific/American Institute at NYU. For more [Ray Ban outlet](http://www.raybanoutletes.com/ "Ray Ban outlet") information or to RSVP go to: www.apa.nyu.edu

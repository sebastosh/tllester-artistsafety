[![](images/BFT_NYT-300x170.jpg "BFT_NYT")](http://freedimensional.org/2011/01/belarus-free-theatre-in-new-york/bft_nyt/)[Belarus Free Theatre](http://www.dramaturg.org) is performing at the [Under the Radar](http://www.undertheradarfestival.com/) festival this week in New York.  fD joined [Trans Europe Halles](http://www.teh.net) to launch a [solidarity fund](http://www.teh.net//tabid/176//Itemid/221/Default.aspx) for Belarus Free Theatre back after the December 19th elections when their members were either in hiding or in jail. freeDimensional worked with [Arts & Democracy](http://www.statevoices.org/artsdem) to publish a [Bridge Conversation with Natalia and Nikolai](http://www.statevoices.org/node/7832), co-founders of the Theatre,  drawn from our October 2010 [Political Salon](http://campaign.r20.constantcontact.com/render?llr=mm9tyicab&v=001ZxTRMtTNNkgUedf2BsgetXaT0x-sRzdkltU-uHJS7ASSZCKSIF-mkQEBiNvxPXFs5MhkpQqexrpQiBVPBR6m6QN96iPomT1qn64YDhx_DHoVqMhMQJcKY9G1e8UyWoHlZb3tAtyvvwo%3D) hosted by the World Policy Institute. [Mischief + Mayhem Books](http://www.mischiefandmayhembooks.com/category/free-belarus/) continued their readings around the US in support of the Belarus Free Theatre, and on Wednesday, January 19th - the day before they travel back to Belarus - [PEN American Center](http://www.pen.org/) is hosting a [celebrity reading on their behalves at Le Poisson Rouge in NYC.](http://lepoissonrouge.com/events/view/1950)

Abdelbaki is the second artist in the New York City Safe Haven Prototype, a multi-organizational residency supporting artists at risk, launched in 2017. The program is supported by ArtistSafety.net, Artistic Freedom Initiative, Westbeth Artists Housing, and Residency Unlimited.

His practice examines racism, religion and politics and their impact on humanity. He describes his work: "I am speaking to the fear inside all of us that keeps us awake...I put a monocle around the open eye to draw attention...painted with striped garments in empty cells...I also see light in that darkness, so I use colors that feel joyous to me. There is hope."
